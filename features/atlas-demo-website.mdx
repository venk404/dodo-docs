**Key Points**:

- **URL**: [atlas.dodopayments.com](https://atlas.dodopayments.com/)
- This is an **AI art generation demo** - users generate images from text prompts
- **Three separate flows** are demonstrated:
    1. **Usage-Based Billing Flow** (Pay per image - $0.75)
    2. **Subscription Product Flow** (Unlimited Pro - $20/month)
    3. **One-Time Purchase Flow** (Credit packs - $7 for 10 credits)
- After completing a purchase, users are redirected to the **Atlas Studio dashboard** where they can generate artwork and track their usage.

## Purchase Flows

### Usage-Based Billing Flow

1. **Select Usage-Based Pricing**: Choose the **pay-per-image** option ($0.75 per image) for AI art generation.
2. **Generate Artwork**: Enter a prompt and generate an image. The system tracks usage automatically.
3. **Checkout**:
    - The user proceeds to Dodo Payments' **usage-based billing checkout** page with Merchant brand name and logo visible.
    - User details and billing address have to be entered and post confirmation, taxes are calculated and total amount and payment details are displayed
    - Enter test card details and make a payment to simulate transaction
4. **Payment Confirmation**: Post-payment, Atlas displays a **confirmation** with usage details and remaining credits.
5. **Dashboard Redirect**: Users are redirected back to the **Atlas Studio dashboard** where they can continue generating artwork and see their usage tracking.

### Subscription Flow

1. **Select a Subscription Product**: Choose a monthly or annual plan for **Unlimited Pro** subscription ($20/month).
2. **Generate Artwork**: Enter a prompt and generate an image. The system tracks usage automatically.
3. **Checkout**:
    - The user proceeds to Dodo Payments' **subscription checkout** page with Merchant brand name and logo visible.
    - User details and billing address have to be entered and post confirmation, taxes are calculated and total amount and payment details are displayed
    - Enter test card details and make a payment to simulate transaction
4. **Payment Confirmation**: Post-payment, Atlas displays a **confirmation** with subscription details.
5. **Dashboard Redirect**: Users are redirected back to the **Atlas Studio dashboard** with unlimited access to generate artwork.

### One-Time Purchase Flow

1. **Select One-Time Purchase Option**: Choose a **Credit Pack** ($7 for 10 credits) for AI art generation.
2. **Generate Artwork**: Enter a prompt and generate an image. The system tracks usage automatically.
3. **Checkout**:
    - The user is taken to the **one-time purchase** checkout flow powered by Dodo Payments with Merchant branding visible.
    - User details and billing address have to be entered and post confirmation, taxes are calculated and total amount and payment details are displayed
    - Enter test card details and make a payment to simulate transaction
4. **Payment Confirmation**: Post-payment, Atlas displays a **confirmation** and user sees a "thank you" screen.
5. **Dashboard Redirect**: Users are redirected back to the **Atlas Studio dashboard** where their purchased credits are available for generating artwork.

## Post-Purchase Dashboard Experience

One highlight of the Atlas demo is that it **tracks billing and usage** for the AI art generation platform:

- **Dashboard Overview**: After completing a purchase, users are redirected to the **Atlas Studio dashboard** where they can immediately start generating artwork.
- **Usage Tracking**: The dashboard displays **remaining credits**, **active subscriptions**, and **generation history**, demonstrating how to implement billing-aware features.
- **Checkout Integration**: This showcases how merchants can integrate Dodo Payments' checkout flows and redirect users back to their application with proper entitlement tracking.
